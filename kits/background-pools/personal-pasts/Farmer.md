> You know how to work the land and make it grow. Your skill and labour feeds the world, yet you are often seen as simple, and perhaps you are a little bit. After all, your focus for many years has been the most basic of production - as well as the most important. From the mighty lord to the lowly beggar, everyone must eat, and eat well. To many this piece of wisdom is a simplistic truism, but that’s just because they don’t quite understand agriculture the way you do.
